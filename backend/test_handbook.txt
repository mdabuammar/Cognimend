Employee Handbook 2026

Vacation Policy:
- Employees receive 20 days of paid vacation annually
- Additional 5 days for employees with 5+ years of service
- Vacation time can be carried over up to 5 days per year

Sick Leave:
- 12 days of paid sick leave per year
- Can be used for medical appointments and family illness
- Requires manager notification within 2 hours

Remote Work:
- Full remote work allowed (5 days per week)
- Optional office days for team meetings
- Core hours: 10 AM - 3 PM EST

Benefits:
- Health insurance covering 85% of premiums
- 401(k) matching up to 6% of salary
- Professional development budget of ,500/year
- Paid parental leave (12 weeks)
